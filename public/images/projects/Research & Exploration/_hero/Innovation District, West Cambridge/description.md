# Innovation District, West Cambridge

## Overview
A masters thesis. The thesis begins with a simple observation: in most innovation campuses, learning and working exist in separate buildings with no designed reason to cross between them. This project is the antidote. Four programmatic wings — education, co-working and startups, library, and culture — are physically connected by sky bridges that make the gap between disciplines visible and then eliminate it. The architecture becomes the mechanism for creative collision, not a container for it.
