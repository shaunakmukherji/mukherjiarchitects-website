# Bresso Airport

## Overview
A solo bachelor thesis. A proposal for the future of Bresso Airfield on the edge of Milan — envisioning what urban aviation infrastructure becomes as electric vertical take-off aircraft and private aviation converge on the same site. The project combines a private jet terminal with a vertiport and asks what architecture for this new category of mobility should feel like: a form that communicates velocity, lightness, and precision in a city that has always treated design as non-negotiable.
