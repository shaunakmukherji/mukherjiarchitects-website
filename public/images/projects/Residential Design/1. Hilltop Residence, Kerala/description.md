# Hilltop Residence, Kerala

## Overview
A private residence designed to minimise its footprint on a forested hillside. The main volume is lifted on stilts so that the forest continues uninterrupted beneath the building; courtyards are carved through the floor plate so that the trees preserved in the process grow up through the architecture itself. The result is a house where the boundary between interior and forest dissolves — where you live inside the nature the building refused to destroy.
