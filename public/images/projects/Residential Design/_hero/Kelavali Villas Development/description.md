# Kelavali Villas Development

## Overview
Two hundred villas, but not two hundred unique buildings. The project proposes a modular system of prototype configurations, each adaptable to the personality of its buyer — introverted arrangements that turn inward around private courtyards, extroverted arrangements that open outward to views and landscape. The developer benefits from economies of scale; the buyer receives a personalised home. The outcome is an estate with the coherence of Santorini — every building speaks the same language, and the collective whole becomes something greater than any individual villa.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Kelavali, Maharashtra, India
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Residential Design
