# Ansari Bungalow, Mumbai

## Overview
The massing was predetermined by setbacks, height limits, and an approved plan. The architecture happened entirely on the facade. The mandatory distribution of windows was treated as a compositional opportunity — the pattern of openings reads like a Mondrian grid, and from that grid a sophisticated play of depth, materiality, and frame was developed. Built and standing in Mumbai: a jewel in the skyline that came from working with what existed rather than against it.
