# Krishna Garden — Housing, Mumbai

## Overview
A slum redevelopment and mid-income housing project in Mumbai. The stepped massing strategy does several things at once: it produces a compelling form in the skyline, introduces light and ventilation into floors that a conventional slab tower would deny, and allows the three wings — studio, 1BHK, and 2BHK — to read as distinct volumes within a coherent whole. Density and desirability, usually in opposition, resolved through section.

- **Location:** Mumbai, India
- **Year:** 2025
