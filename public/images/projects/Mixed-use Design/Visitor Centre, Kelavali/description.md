# Visitor Centre, Kelavali

## Overview
Designed from the start for two lives. During the sales phase, it gives prospective buyers their first experience of the development from the highest point on the site — cantilevered decks projecting over the valley, the full 200-villa estate laid out below. When the last villa sells, the building converts to a restaurant: the views, the deck, the cantilever, and the elevated position were planned for that second life from day one. One building, two purposes, no compromise.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Kelavali, Maharashtra, India
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Mixed-use Design
