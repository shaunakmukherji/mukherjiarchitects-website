# Sales Pavilion, Kelavali

## Overview
The brief was simple: help sell 200 villas. The architecture responded by treating the sales process as a ceremonial journey rather than a transaction. Visitors move clockwise around a central courtyard through a carefully sequenced series of spaces — introduction, body, conclusion — each room a chapter in a story that culminates in the decision to buy. The building demonstrates the quality of the development it is selling, offering a spatial preview of the hospitality and craft that buyers are committing to.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Kelavali, Maharashtra, India
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Mixed-use Design
