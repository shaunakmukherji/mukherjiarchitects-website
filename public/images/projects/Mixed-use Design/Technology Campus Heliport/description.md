# Technology Campus Heliport

## Overview
Every dimension of this structure was determined before a single line was drawn. Aviation safety regulations prescribe the clearance radius around the landing pad, the angle of approach, and the envelope of permissible obstruction. These parameters were encoded and used to shape the built form from the inside out — producing a ribbed steel and glass canopy that feels inevitable rather than designed. Positioned at the entrance to the campus, visible on arrival, it announces a technology environment through the very process by which it was made.
