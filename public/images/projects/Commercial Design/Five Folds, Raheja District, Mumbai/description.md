# Five Folds, Raheja District, Mumbai

## Overview
Five restaurant kiosks within an upcoming mixed-use district in Navi Mumbai, defined by a signature folding geometry — hence the name. Staggered volumes and strong angular forms build identity across the cluster, while solar-powered canopy structures shade the outdoor alfresco dining areas and simultaneously power each unit. Lightweight, self-sufficient, and architecturally coherent.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Navi Mumbai, India
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Commercial Design
