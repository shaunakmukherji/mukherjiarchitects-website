# Open-Air Retail Pavilion, New Cairo, Egypt

## Overview
A competition entry proposing a fundamentally different model for how retail generates revenue — not through branding volume, but through architectural control of pedestrian behaviour. Car parking was deliberately limited and repositioned to put visitors on foot through a curated spatial sequence, slowing movement, extending dwell time, and increasing spend. A unified neoclassical language was applied across the entire development, subordinating individual brand identities to create a premium destination organised around a landscaped central courtyard.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** New Cairo, Egypt
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Commercial Design
