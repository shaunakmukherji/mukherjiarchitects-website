# Six & Six Island Resort, Maldives

## Overview
The most ambitious project in the studio's portfolio. Positioned directly against the finest resorts in the Maldives — the place that defines the ceiling of luxury hospitality worldwide — Six & Six sets out to be number one in the category that is already number one. The full scope spans architecture, interiors, and masterplanning. The design draws from the extraordinary aquatic ecology of the site, translating the fluid geometry of coral formations and marine life into an architectural language shaped through parametric design. Currently under construction.

- **Location:** Maldives
- **Year:** 2025
