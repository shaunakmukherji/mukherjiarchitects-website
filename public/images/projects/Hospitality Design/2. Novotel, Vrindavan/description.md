# Novotel, Vrindavan

## Overview
A hotel under construction in one of India's most sacred cities, designed as a wedding destination where classical architecture is not a stylistic choice but a first-principles response to context. Symmetry, proportion, and alignment govern every decision — from overall massing to the arrangement of keys — ensuring the building meets its full operational brief without sacrificing architectural authority. A form that cannot go wrong because it was never designed to be fashionable.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Vrindavan, India
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Hospitality Design
