# Taj, Guwahati

## Overview
The challenge was to deliver a full resort experience on a constrained urban site where horizontal sprawl was not an option. The answer was to stack the resort vertically — rethinking every typological assumption about how luxury hospitality is organised, landscaped, and experienced floor by floor. The design resolves climate, FSI maximisation, and room count simultaneously, while ensuring that architecture and landscaping alone justify a premium rate per key.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Guwahati, India
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Hospitality Design
