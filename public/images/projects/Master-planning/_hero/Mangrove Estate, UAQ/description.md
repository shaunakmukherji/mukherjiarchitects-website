# Mangrove Estate, UAQ

## Overview
A masterplan proposal presented directly to the Sheikh of Umm al-Quwain for the development of a significant coastal site. Rather than a conventional grid, a radial planning strategy was chosen — concentric zones radiating outward from a central cultural and community heart, producing a plan that reads as land art from the air. Each ring carries its own programmatic identity, density, and character, creating a perfect gradient from civic centre to waterfront edge. Ordered, legible, and beautiful at every scale.

## Technical Details
- **Location:** Umm Al Quwain, UAE
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Master Planning
